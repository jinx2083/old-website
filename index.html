Does the book "The Secret" really work?

By Samira Gadri | Sept 5th, 2018

The Secrety by Rhonda Byrnes is a classic self-development book. It is frequently referenced by athletes and other individuals as a tool of their success.

Lessons from the book

  • The secret is the law of attraction
  • You attract what you think about
  • Yo change your life, change your thoughts
  • The law of attraction is in-discriminatory, the reason many people don't succeed is because they fixate on the things they don't want and the universe gives them more of this
  • Think of thoughts as literally leading to creation, because they actually do; your current thoughts are molding your future
  • Meditation is the best way to become aware of your thoughts
  • This explains why affirmations are so powerful
  • Make your last thoughts before going to sleep good ones
  • Affirmations to practice: "my thoughts become things", "I am a master of my thoughts"
  • Take complete responsibility for everything that happens in your life, even seemingly accidents
  • "Nothing can come into your experience unless you summon it through persistent thoughts"
  • It is difficult to monitor all of the thoughts which you have throughout a day, so observe your feelings, as they are signals to the type of thought frequencies dominating your mind
  • It is impossible to feel bad and at the same time have good thoughts, and vice versa
  • If you feel bad, change your thoughts to good thoughts
  • Secret Shifters: a list of things which instantly lift your mood (e.g. music, nature, loved-one, a memory, etc.)
  • By the law of attraction, if you think that life is hard and a struggle, you will experience life as hard and a struggle
  • affirmations to practice: "This is a magnificent Universe", "I deserve all good things life has to offer"
